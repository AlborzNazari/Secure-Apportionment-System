The Huntington-Hill Method

Also called the method of equal proportions, this algorithm minimizes the relative difference in representation between any two parties. It has been used to apportion seats in the U.S. House of Representatives since 1941.

✓ Avoids the Alabama Paradox
✓ Minimizes relative inequality
